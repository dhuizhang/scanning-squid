qcodes-squid¶

Scanning SQUID measurement & control based on QCoDeS.¶

qcodes-squid is an instrument control and data acquisition package for scanning SQUID (Superconducting QUantum Interference Device) microscopy developed in the Moler Group at Stanford University. It is based on the QCoDeS data acquisition framework.
